Guacamole

This will be a training video

Instructions

  1. Wash the avocados.
  2. Cut and peel avocados and take out pits. Put avocado in a bowl.
  3. Add about 6oz of salsa. This will help to smooth the avocado.
  4. In the bowl, smash the avocados until smooth.
  5. Cut the lime in half and squeeze out the juice into the bowl.
  6. Cut the red onion and add to bowl.
  7. Add incrementally garlic powder as desired. Beware not to change taste too much.
  8. Optional: add 1-2 oz of 7 up, which adds sweetness
  9. Add salt as desired.

Ingredients

  • Avocados: 4-6 (make sure they are soft)
  • Salsa: 1 jar
  • Garlic Powder
  • Lime: 1-2
  • Red Onion: 1
  • Salt
  • Optional: 7-up (6 oz)

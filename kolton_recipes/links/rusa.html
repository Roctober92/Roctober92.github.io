La Ensalada Rusa

This will be a training video

Instructions

  1. Start 2 pots of water, put on 7-8 high
  2. In one pot, put in the eggs, and fill water until it barely covers all eggs
  3. Chop potatoes into half-inch cubes
  4. Wash potatoes in a strainer
  5. Add potatoes to water, add salt

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: 2-4 (less if they are bigger)
  • Mayonnaise: 1 jar
  • Peas: 1 can
  • (Optional) Carrots: 2-3 chopped

Baked Fish

This will be a training video

Since common sides to this dish are considered more or less simple (rice, noodles, vegetables), I'll only focus on the fish.

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 425
  2. Cut cookie sheet sized tin foil cut, place on cookie sheet
  3. Place filet on tin foil
  4. Lightly sprinkle any seasoning wanted on the fish
  5. Pour about 1 tablespoon of honey on the fish
  6. Place 2-4 tablespoons of sauce on the filet
  7. Spread sauce till filet is covered
  8. Fold foil around filet to cover the filet
  9. When oven is heated, put sheet with foil wrapped fish in the oven
  10. Depending on the size of the fish, tilapia takes about 15 minutes, atlantic salmon takes about 25
  11. When fish is done, all types will be easy to cut, and mostly white, completely absent of rawish pink
  12. Enjoy with favorite side!

Ingredients

  • Fish: 1lb slice (suggestions are atlantic salmon, or tilapia)
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Honey
  • Tin Foil
  • Optional: Garlic and Herb Seasoning
  • Optional: Lemon Seasoning

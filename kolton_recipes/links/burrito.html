Breakfast Burritos

This will be a training video

Instructions

  1. Hours Before: Prepare the potato
    • Cube potato into about 1/2 inch size each
    • Wash off in strainer
    • Put in already warming water (7 high), add salt
    • Let boil
    • Try after about 7-10 minutes, put back in strainer when cubes are soft
    • After cooling off, store in fridge until ready
  2. Everything else when ready
  3. Chop bell pepper and onion
  4. Add both to skillet with a little oil, to sauteé (6-7 heat)
  5. Chop meat into cubes or slices 1/2 inch in size
  6. Add meat into skillet when pepper and onion are starting to crisp
  7. Sprinkle garlic salt, enough to barely cover all
  8. Occasionally stir, until meat is cooked
  9. Add oil throughout as needed
  10. Add in chilled potato cubes
  11. Dice tomato into small cubes
  12. On the side, mix eggs with a tablespoon of milk
  13. After potatoes start to turn brown, add in tomatoes and eggs
  14. Keep stirring and turning until eggs are cooked
  15. Take off burner
  16. Enjoy with tortillas, cheese, and any other sauces

Ingredients

  • Tortillas: 6-8 medium sized suggested
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bell Pepper: 1
  • Onion: 1
  • Eggs: 6
  • Potato: 1
  • Tomato: 1
  • Meat: 1.5 lb, Chicken breast or bratwürst suggested, any meat that is sauteéable
  • Sour Cream
  • Garlic Salt
  • Optional: Cheese
  • Optional: Hot Sauce

Instructions

  1. Thinly slice the chicken. You want it as thin as possible. You can buy thinly sliced chicken, but it usually isn't thin enough.
  2. In a plate, mix bread crumbs with garlic salt and any other spices like black pepper
  3. Crack 3 eggs, whip, and put onto 2nd plate.
  4. For every chicken slice...
  5. Bathe the slice in the egg until all wet
  6. Flip the slice around in the bread crumbs. Press down on the slice with strength, as if you were to flatten it. Make sure crumbs get stuck to all sides
  7. After all slices have been breaded, put enough oil in pan until bottom is thinly covered. Turn on heat (4-5), and heat oil until crackling starts.
  8. Put 3-5 slices into pan. Turn over and over again until all sides are brown
  9. When brown, use tongs to take chicken out, and quickly put it under running colder water for a split second, which crispens chicken and washed off excess oil.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast: 2 Slices (about 1.5 lbs)
  • Eggs: 3-5
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Garlic Salt
  • Vegetable/Corn Oil
  • Optional: Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. If you want meat: dice the bell pepper and the onion
  2. Cube the meat
  3. Saute√© the diced veggies in a skillet on medium (6-7) heat for a few minutes, until starting to crisp slightly
  4. Add cubed meat into skillet
  5. Add garlic salt and other select seasonings a gusto, (which means as you wish)
  6. Occasionally stir until skillet contents are thoroughly cooked
  7. On the side: Scramble the eggs mixed with milk
  8. When eggs are done, combine back with other skillet
  9. Put all contents to the side
  10. Put about one spoonfull of oil into skillet, and sprinkle with garlic salt, on 4-5 heat
  11. After about 3-4 minutes, put on tortilla, contents, and cheese, and evenly spread across tortilla
  12. After sitting for a few minutes, or until bottom of tortilla is crispening, flip tortilla once to make semi-cirle.
  13. Flip over as many times as needed, but should cook quickly
  14. Take out of skillet, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas: However many you want
  • Corn/Vegetable Oil
  • Garlic Salt
  • Any kind of cheese you want (hand shredded cheddar suggested)
  • Optional: 1lb Meat (any kind of steak/ground beef/chicken will do)
  • Optional: 3-6 Eggs
  • Optional: 1-2 Tomatoes
  • Optional: 1 Green/Red bell pepper
  • Optional: 1 Onion
  • Optional: select seasoning for the meat (different kinds for each meat available at store)
  • Optional: 1/4 cup milk

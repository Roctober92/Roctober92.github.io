Happiness

2 Nephi 5

"And we did observe to keep the judgements, and the statutes, and the commandments of the Lord in all things... And the Lord was with us; and we did prosper exceedingly; for we did sow seed ... reap again in abundance ... raise flocks, and herds, and animals of every kind ... build buildings ... work in all manner of wood, and of iron, of copper ... to be industrious ... And it came to pass that we lived after the manner of happiness."

Mosiah 2:41

"... I would desire that ye should consider the blessed and happy state of those that keep the commandments of God. For behold, they are blessed in all things, both temporal and spiritual; and if they hold out faithful to the end they are received into heaven, that thereby they may dwell with God in a state of never-ending happiness."

4 Nephi

"...and there were no contentions and disputations ... every man did deal justly with one another ... they had all things common among them ... and they were married, and given in marriage ... they did walk after the commandments which they had received from their Lord and their God ... no envyings, nor strifes, nor tumults, nor lyings, nor whoredoms ... and and surely there could not be a happier people among all the people who had been created by the hand of God."

Personal Insight

Happiness is the object of our design, and I believe the drive behind all behavior. These three passages highlight brilliantly three dimensions of being happy. It involves having purposeful work, obeying God's commandments (which is avoiding pitfalls), and having Godly attributes one towards another.

The happiest I've been in my life are in times where I had a focus on others. I was constantly thinking what to do next to help them. In other times, I would be involved in a purposeful cause, whether in study or in work. And not just any work makes me happy, but the kind that has a positive affect on other people, and involves my critical thinking.

God has a fulness of joy. I think it comprises being self-reliant, having good purposes in everything, extending talents, and thinking of other as yourself. Happiness is had despite of situation.

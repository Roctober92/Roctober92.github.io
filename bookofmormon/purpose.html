Purpose

Alma 12:24, 31

" ... this life became a probationary state; a time to prepare to meet God ... he gave commandments unto men ... and becoming Gods, knowing good from evil, placing themselves in a state to act, or being placed in a state to act according to their wills and pleasures, whether to do evil or to do good."

Alma 31:31-34

"now is the time and the day of your salvation; and therefore, if ye will repent and harden not your hearts ... this life is the time for men to prepare to meet God; yea, behold the day of this life is the day for men to perform their labors ... I beseech of you that ye do not procrastinate the day of your repentance until the end; for after this day of life, which is given us to prepare for eternity, behold, if we do not improve our time while in this life, then cometh the night of darkness wherein there can be no labor performed."

2 Nephi 2:21, 25

"And the days of the children of men were prolonged ... that they might repent while in the flesh; wherefore, their state became a state of probation, and their time was lengthened, according to the commandments which the Lord God gave unto the children of men ... Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy ... they have become free forever, knowing good from evil; to act for themselves and not to be acted upon."

Personal Insight

Praise God for giving us in such sweet lines, our purpose. The Book of Mormon is a marvelous creation, and has helped me find purpose in life. This is our time to perform our labors, to show unto God we do care. He is giving us this time to develop Christlike attributes, to make a difference in the lives of people, enrich our testimonies, develop talents, and enjoy.

I was once told in a blessing that this life is the perfect time for me to make and learn from mistakes. That is its design. I love that if we aren't sure about somethings in this life, we can try it out a little, and know by experience it's value.

Charity

Moroni 7: 45

And charity suffereth long, and is kind, and envieth not, and is not puffed up, seeketh not her own, is not easily provoked, thinketh no evil, and rejoiceth not in iniquity, but rejoiceth in the truth, beareth all things, believeth all things, hopeth all things, endureth all things.

Moroni 7:46-47

Wherefore, my beloved brethren, if ye have not charity, ye are nothing, for charity never faileth. Wherefore, cleave unto charity, which is the greatest of all, for all things must fail... But charity is the pure love of Christ, and it endureth forever; and whoso is found possessed of it at the last day, it shall be well with him.

Moroni 7:48

Wherefore, my beloved brethren, pray unto the Father with all the energy of heart, that ye may be filled with this love, which he hath bestowed upon all who are true followers of his Son, Jesus Christ; that ye may become the sons of God; that when he shall appear we shall be like him, for we shall see him as he is; that we may have this hope; that we may be purified even as he is pure.

Personal Insights

What does it mean to pray with all of the enery of our hearts? I've experienced being "filled with his love" before, and it is marvelous. When your thoughts are consumed with meaningful thoughts of helping others, passionate about their welfare, consuming priority even above other normal activites and functions.

It takes effort to think positively about others, ignoring all negative thoughts, and praying for them, and for charity. But being willing is the key. And of course it lays at the root for all your do and think about, but it sets all of your thoughts and desires in a straight and narrow course. Wouldn't it be nice to be free of envy, anger, negatism, and fear?

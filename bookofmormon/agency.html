Agency

2 Nephi 2:16

"Wherefore, the Lord God gave unto man that he should act for himself...he hath created all things, both the heavens and earth, and all things that in them are, both things to act and things to be acted upon...men...have become free forever, knowing good from evil; to act for themselves and not to be acted upon..."

Helaman 14:30-31

"And now remember, remember, my brethren, that whosoever perisheth, perisheth unto himself; and whosoever doeth iniquity, doeth unto himself' for behold, ye are free; ye are permitted to act for yourselfs; ...God hath given unto a knowledge and he hath made you free...that ye might choose life or death."

2 Nephi 10:23

"Therefore, cheer up your hearts, and remember that ye are free to act for yourselves-to choose the way of everlasting death or the way of eternal life."

Personal Insight

As it says in Abraham: "...Satan rebelled against me, and sought to destroy of agency of man, which I, the Lord God, had given him, and also, that I should give unto him mine own power". Agency is key on God's plan. How are we to progress if we don't choose? Elder Bednar once said that the best teachers didn't give him the answers, but led him in a way so that he could find his own answers. Simply we choose our destiny, we choose who we become. There is no other way. God lets us become who we want, regardless of what he wants. He won't control us, like Satan wants, because that is opposite of progression's natural laws.

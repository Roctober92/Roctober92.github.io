The Metal Straw

  • It's called "La bombilla".
  • It has a filter at the rounded bottom part, made up of little holes. This allows the brewed juice to pass, but not the mate yerba particles.

Drinking Tips

  • Do not move the straw once water is poured: it makes the mate particles enter the straw.
  • Add sugar and honey. Otherwise, it will be bitter.

Pizza and Empanadas

Pizza tastes different in Argentina. Less sauce, the cheese has a bold taste. In Pizza restaurants, you'll find a variety of toppings that you won't in the U.S (hot dogs, french fry chips, corn, and fried eggs). However, it is generally much more healthy.

Empanadas are delicious and famous! They are like hot-pockets, but with homemade breading, closed around the edges in a braided style with your finger-tips. They are filled generally with beef, chicken, and ham. Select locations will have more creative fillings.

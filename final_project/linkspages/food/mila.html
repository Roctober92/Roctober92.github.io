Author's Note:

As a missionary, I always was given lunch by the local members. We were probably given milanesa 40% of the time, especially in the summer, when it was too hot for pasta dishes. Everyone loves milanesa. We never got tired of it. It sold for about 70 pesos a kilogram, and the sandwiches in public places sold for about 50 pesos with toppings.

What is my website?

This website will teach the reader how to "become" more Argentine. It will explain ways which the reader can do Argentine things, and benefit from their cultural lifestyle

Description of Nav-bar items, the 5 pages

  • Mate Mate is the drink of Argentina. This section will talk about what mate is, why it's important to Argentina, and how to drink it.
  • Habits This will explain their peculiar but wonderful habits, like daily schedule, hand-speech, trash-burning techniques, social skills, etc.
  • Food This section will highlight the Argentine food selection. It will talk a little about each food, and provide a recipe and instruction part for a best dish.
  • Culture This will include information about their holidays, trains, supermarkets, way of traveling to work, etc.
  • Speech This will include instructions and explanations on how to sound like a Buenos Aires Capital naticve (Porte√±o)
